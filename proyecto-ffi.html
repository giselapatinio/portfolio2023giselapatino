1. PROBLEM

"We lack a modern and digital experience due to the absence of a usable website."

Time

DIC 2020

Team

3 people’s

Service

UX/UI Design

This was a collaborative project with the UX Agency Dese Estudio, based in Argentina. The task was to revamp the user experience for the FFI Law Firm.

2. PAIN POINTS

"First, we gather information and establish an agile process for viable solutions."

For this journey with Dese, we used the Design Thinking methodology, which highlighted four key aspects missing from the digital experience within the justice space.

3. RESULTS

"The research revealed a valuable opportunity for the industry: most studios lack an accessible and responsive website."

Goals & Briefing

Our goal was to provide an ecosystem of interactive and digital experiences that lead to a website for managing everything 100% online. We created a responsive website, interactive PDF guides with social media links and cards with QR codes that lead to a multilink, allowing individuals to choose their preferred action.

Defining our buyer persona

We identified the four main audiences, allowing us to create a consistent and unified style in everything FFI communicates, displays, and responds to the needs of each segment.

Point of view

So, we created user stories and empathy maps that intersect with the reality of the times at each stage of the process. This demonstrated to us that service excellence occurs when the experience aligns with the type of buyer persona.

Journey map & flows

After analyzing the digital maturity of the target audience, FFI established internal guidelines to offer service spaces aligned with the reality of each person approaching the firm. Moreover, through data analysis, they gained insights into deploying tools and resources that facilitated UX practices for the studio.

New Design System Banco Macro

I established the inaugural web design system to implement the bank's new aesthetic guidelines. To achieve this, we analyzed information architecture, commercial and institutional communication spaces, and components of the Oracle CMS. The project entailed revamping certain areas and creating new ones to expedite implementation timelines.

Jubis

We have worked collaboratively with the product area to improve the segment experience. The main objective was to redesign a site adapted to the new identity and with the Inclusite accessibility tool to make it accessible.
