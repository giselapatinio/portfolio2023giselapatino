2. PAIN POINTS

“First, we gather information and establish an agile process for viable solutions.”

The greatest challenge I faced was consolidating the UX discipline into decision-making, implementing its best practices and agile processes, in order to have more relevant web content for both the user and the business.

3. RESULTS

"You're on the verge of witnessing a whole year filled with a spectrum of emotions, learning, experiences, and most importantly, a strong sense of camaraderie."

Home Page of Banco Macro

We revamped the bank's official webpage with fresh visual elements and accessibility tools. Our mission was to modernize and streamline the interface of the Oracle CMS to enhance the experience. We worked on the text, restructured communication spaces, and devised a new design system.

Card Acquisition Flow

I collaborated closely with Poincenot to enhance the user flow for product acquisition. Together, we achieved delivering a digital, responsive, and secure experience. Additionally, we identified pain points in credit card acquisition to offer positive communication spaces with valuable information for the users.

Rapicompra by Banco Macro

The issue with this banking proposal was that users weren't utilizing the benefits businesses offered with the bank. So, we realized that the solution was to enhance the content quality and the reception page experience. To achieve that, we crafted clearer and more appealing digital elements to gain better visibility of partnerships.

Job Fair in Argentina

Every year, the bank participates in expos to promote youth employment. To accomplish this, we revamped the image and application experience on the Success Factors platform. Despite facing design limitations, our emphasis was on enhancing the accessibility of the application process.

New Design System Banco Macro

I established the inaugural web design system to implement the bank's new aesthetic guidelines. To achieve this, we analyzed information architecture, commercial and institutional communication spaces, and components of the Oracle CMS. The project entailed revamping certain areas and creating new ones to expedite implementation timelines.
